What We Offer

Comprehensive Study Material

To help you stay on top of the game, our most thorough study material is compiled by subject matter specialists and gives you a deep understanding of all the important themes from a variety of areas.

Excellent Track Record

For more than a half decade, we have consistently delivered remarkable outcomes in competitive exams, board exams for the ICSE and CBSE, and other assessments.

Periodic Tests

At Parivartan Classes, we administer a weekly test on the subjects covered throughout the whole week of instruction. The topic teacher has carefully selected and vetted the test questions.

Individual Attention to Each Student

We teach limited number of students in each of our batch. With this we can easily monitor how a student is performing and what more needs to delivered from our end as well.
